Hawaiian Sausage Skillet Recipe

A custom skillet thrown together. Easy to adjust ingredients while staying fresh and tasty.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you will need to make this recipe:

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  • 1 (16 ounce) package of Polish sausage.
  • 1 large, thinly sliced onion.
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch squares.
  • 3/4 fresh pineapple. Make sure it is peeled, cored, and cut into chunks.
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch.
  • 1/2 cup of pineapple juice.
  • 1/3 cup of packaged brown sugar.
  • 2 tablespoons of cider vinegar.
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic.

Steps

Follow these steps below to make this homemade chicken lasagna:

  • Gather your ingredients.
  • Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir sausage, onion, and green bell pepper until vegetables start to become tender or about 8 minutes. Add pineapple and cook, stirring occasionally, until the pineapple is hot for about 5 minutes more.
  • Beat pineapple juice and cornstarch together in a bowl using a whisk. Stir brown sugar, cider vinegar, soy sauce, and minced garlic with the pineapple juice until the sugar dissolves into the liquid. Pour over the sausage mixture and cook until the sauce thickens, 5 to 8 minutes.
